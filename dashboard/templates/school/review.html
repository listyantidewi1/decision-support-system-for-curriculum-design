Multi-reviewer & crowd-sourced Each reviewer’s feedback is stored separately (per reviewer_id). Schools with the same vocational field can aggregate results on the Results page to benefit from crowd-sourced data across departments.

How feedback is used: When multiple reviewers assess the same item, majority vote merges feedback. Your feedback is applied in Phase 2 (Run Phase 2 after review) to correct Bloom/type, calibrate thresholds, and re-generate competencies.

Limitations: Skills, knowledge, and competencies are stratified samples; not all items are reviewed. Unverified items may still appear in final results.
