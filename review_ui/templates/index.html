Review Rubric — when to choose Valid/Invalid, Quality 1–5, Relevant?

Skills & Knowledge — Valid?

Valid: Real skill/knowledge from the job text; actionable or meaningful concept. Invalid: Garbage, fragment, hallucination, not a skill/knowledge, or degree requirement. When unsure, choose Invalid.

Competency — Quality (1–5)

1 Poor: Unusable (incoherent, wrong grouping). 2 Fair: Weak. 3 Good: Acceptable, usable. 4–5: Strong to excellent, curriculum-ready.

Competency — Relevant?

Relevance = alignment with related skills + curriculum domain. Yes: Competency accurately groups skills and is useful for curriculum. Partial: Some fit, some don't. No: Misaligned with skills or not relevant to domain.
